Crash Detection and Automatic Emergency Response

Our sophisticated impact and crash detection system determines when a rider has been involved in an accident that is likely to cause serious bodily harm. It automatically notifies a designated emergency contact who can then request emergency services to your exact location and provide relevant medical information.

Theft Prevention and Property Recovery

Our anti-theft measures include the standard line of defense, but give you some extra advantages. Not only is our system capable of emitting a loud alarm as a deterrent, it notifies you by text message and smartphone app notification whenever someone is tampering with your property, which includes your motorcycle and luggage. In the event that someone successfully steals your motorcycle, our system is equipped with a GPS tracker that will allow the authorities to recover your property.
