Getting Started

This page gives you information on getting started with your MotoGuardian and instructions on how to setup and install the device on your vehicle.
Step 1. Connect the longest pair of wires to the power and ground wires leading to the horn using the provide jumper clips. Take care to wire according to the correct polarity.
Step 2. Using the provided adhesive wire clips, route that wire under body panels, along the frame toward the desired mounting location for the device.
Step 3. Route the 2nd longest pair of wires that terminate in connector rings to the battery and connect them. Take care to wire according to the correct polarity.
Step 4. Connect the shortest pair of wires to the provided switch. You will mount this switch somewhere on the motorcycle so that it can be accessed to cancel unnecessary emergency calls.
Step 5. Connect the connector at the end of the wire harness to the device.
Step 6. Use zip-ties to clean up routed cables where clips will not work. Try to make wiring as inconspicuous as possible.
