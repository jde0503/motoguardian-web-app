Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is MotoGuardian?

    • MotoGuardian is an Internet of Things (IOT) device that was developed to improve motocycle security. The device is equipped with an array of sensors that focus on anti-theft and emergency contact capabilities.

  • How much does MotoGuardian cost?

    • The cost for MotoGuardian is still to be determined. Please check back soon!

  • If I crash, will MotoGuardian notify the police and emergency services?

    • No, because of the various legal issues depending on jurisdictions, MotoGuardian will notify a designated emergency contact.

  • Will any data that MotoGuardian collects be sold to third parties (e.g. insurance companies or claims management companies)?

    • No, the MotoGuardian team will never sell any of your data to third parties.

  • Does MotoGuardian track and provide my speed data to law enforcement?

    • This question is one that often causes concern from our customers. MotoGuardian has an opt-in feature to record your speed during your trip for performance analytics. You can easily opt-out of the service - no strings attached. However, MotoGuardian will NOT provide speed data to law enforcement officials.

  • Does MotoGuardian require a subscription for the data connection between the device and the web application?

    • No, there are no subscription fees associated with the MotoGuardian device. You will need to purchase a 2G SIM card from a mobile company (e.g. Ting) for the device to sync data to the web application.
